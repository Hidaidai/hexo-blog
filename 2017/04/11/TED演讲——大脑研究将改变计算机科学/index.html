TED演讲——大脑的研究对计算机科学的影响

Apr 11, 2017 | 计算机方向 | Hits

  开始学习英语的第一篇TED演讲，How brain science will change computing ,讲师是杰夫·霍金斯(Jeff Hawkins) ,全球最大掌上电脑制造商Palm创始人，掌上型电脑PalmPilot、智能电话Treo以及许多手持装置的发明人，《人工智能的未来》一书作者。
  演讲主要内容是关于大脑的研究对计算机科学的影响。

2.My Review

This presentation first introduced Jeff Hawkins’ life experience, which led to his study of the brain.He believes the human brain has a neocortex other than the “old brain” that the general animals have, which makes the human brain have a memory-prediction system.It is this memory-predictive system has a far-reaching significance for the development of artificial intelligence.
I am very strange why a computer researcher in the TED speech told about the human brain. In fact, this research area should be regarded as Perception Science, from the computer science point of view, we can think it is the reverse engineering of the brain, which is a front study direction about artificial intelligence。The Nobel Prize for Medicine in 2014 is related to this. Jeff is very far-sighted, because this TED speech was released in 2003.
His understanding of the human brain is put aside first, but his analysis of the development of artificial intelligence is very thorough. The current intelligent vehicle, intelligent security system is simple response to a situation, like the crocodile’s “old brain”, very complex but not human intelligence. In future ，which artificial intelligence will develop, Jeff did not make predictions, but I think it’s almost like AlphaGo.

中文：
这篇演讲首先介绍了Jeff Hawkins的人生经历，从而引出他对大脑的研究。他认为人脑在普罗大众都具有的”旧脑”之外具有新大脑皮层(neocortex)，这使得人脑具有了记忆-预测系统。也就是这个记忆-预测系统对人工智能发展具有深远的意义。
其实我也很奇怪，为什么一个搞计算机的大佬在TED演讲会讲到人脑。其实这个研究领域严格的说应该算是感知学，跟心理学和行为学关系紧密，从计算机科学角度说，我们也可以叫做大脑的反向工程，是人工智能研究中的一个非常前沿的研究方向。2014年的诺贝尔医学奖就和这个有关。从中可以看出Jeff非常具有远瞻性，因为这篇TED演讲是2003年发布的。
他对于人脑的理解是否正确暂且不谈，但他对人工智能的发展进行的分析非常透彻。目前的智能汽车，智能安保系统处于初期仅仅是就某一情况进行反应，就像鳄鱼的“旧脑”，非常复杂但称不上人类的智能。未来的人工智能会发展到什么程度，Jeff没有进行预测，但我认为差不多就像阿法狗吧。
